Non-Profit Robotics

WHY?

Knowledge

By engineering cool, useful, and innovative products we’re exposing people of all ages to the wonders of technology and providing opportunities to pursue a career in the industry. We provide resources to build your own projects and all of our code is open source all the time so you can take a look at what we did, take it apart and make it your own.

Scholarships

Aren’t interested in building anything? Help somebody take their first steps of their journey to an amazing career. You get something cool and the profits go towards providing educational and occupational opportunities to some very deserving people.

WTF ?!

Last but not least, we’re engineering fun, innovative, and helpful products that help people in many ways both small and large. By doing so we get to test our skills, build some badass stuff, and share what we love to do with you.
