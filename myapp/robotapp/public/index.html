Non-Profit Robotics

WHY?

Knowledge

We’re exposing people of all ages to the wonders of technology by providing the resources to rebuild some of our cool, useful, and innovative products. All of our code is open source all the time so you can take a look at how we did it, take it apart, and make it your own.

Scholarships

Aren’t interested in building anything? Help somebody take the first steps of their journey to an amazing career. You get an awesome robot and the profits go towards providing educational and occupational opportunities to some very deserving people.

WTF ?!

Last but not least, we’re engineering fun, creative, and helpful products that help people in many ways both small and large. By doing so we get to test our skills, build some badass stuff, and share what we love to do with you.

Want a Scholarship?

We don't care about who you are, where you come from, or what grade you got in your P.E. class. If you're interested in pursuing a career in the technology industry, we're here to help whether it's building websites, creating mobile apps, building robots, or anything else you can think of! All you have to do is show us why you deserve it. Build a robot, code some cool shit and send us your github repo, make a video explaining why we should throw fat stacks at you and your future, etc. Whatever it is you do get creative and have fun with it!
